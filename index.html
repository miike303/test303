Controls & Tips

Survive The Swarm

Desktop

  • Move with WASD or Arrow Keys.
  • Space dashes. P pauses.
  • Weapons auto-fire at nearby threats.

Mobile

  • Drag the virtual stick on the lower-left.
  • Tap the Dash button for burst evasion.
  • Menus use large touch-friendly controls.

Run Flow

  • Gather XP cells to level up and choose 1 of 3 upgrades.
  • Difficulty surges over time with elites, mini-bosses, hazards, and bosses.
  • Earn alloy after every run and buy permanent upgrades.
