    How to play

    Designed for thumbs, keyboard, and quick rematches.

    • Watch the sequence and wait for Your turn.
    • Tap pads or use Q/W/A/S to match the pattern.
    • Every completed round adds a new step and speeds up slightly.
    • Strict is faster. Zen lets you retry the current round.
    • Milestones at rounds 5, 10, 15, and 20 award bonuses and celebrations.
