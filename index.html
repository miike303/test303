    How it works

    Simple to start, difficult to master.

    • Press Start Game to hear and watch the sequence.
    • Repeat it by tapping the four pads. Keyboard support also works with Q, W, A, and S.
    • Each cleared round adds one new step and the pace gradually increases.
    • Strict Mode accelerates harder. Zen Practice lets you retry the round after a mistake.
    • Rounds 5, 10, 15, and 20 trigger milestone celebrations and special feedback.
